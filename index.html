About us

The Interior Design Dictionary is a comprehensive bilingual resource that provides terminology in the field of interior design in both Arabic and English. We strive to offer accurate and valuable information for designers and interior design enthusiasts. This project was proudly developed under the supervision of Dr. Sara Fawzi, within the ecological Interior Design Department – Applied Arts, Helwan University.
